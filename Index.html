Instructions & Privacy

Instructions

  1. Ensure your supervisor has reviewed and approved your abstract prior to submission.
  2. Upon submitting your abstract, you will receive a confirmation email with a link to view and edit the contents of your abstract up until the deadline and portal closes.
  3. Once your abstract is reviewed by the Office of Research, you will receive confirmation of not accepted or accepted with further instructions for formatting your poster.

For additional information, visit the FoMD Summer Students' Research Day page.

Privacy Statement

The personal information collected through this form (including name, email address, studentship details, supervisor information, and abstract content) is collected under the authority of Section 33(c) of the Alberta Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FOIP) and the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

This information is collected for the purpose of administering the Faculty of Medicine & Dentistry Summer Students' Research Day abstract review and presentation selection process. Responses will be stored securely and access will be limited to the FoMD Undergraduate Research Program.

If you have any questions about the collection or use of your information, please contact:

FoMD Undergraduate Research Program
Faculty of Medicine & Dentistry, University of Alberta
fmdugrad@ualberta.ca

For more information about the Alberta Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act, visit the Government of Alberta FOIP page.
